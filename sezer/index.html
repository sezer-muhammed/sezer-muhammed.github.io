About Me

I’m Muhammed Sezer - a devoted Electrical & Electronics Engineering student from Middle East Technical University, Ankara.

During my educational journey, I have developed a deep interest in robotics, leading me to contribute to various significant projects, including Swarm Robotic Research, Mobile Robotics, and Rehabilitation Robotics Research. I have also taken the entrepreneurial route by establishing Orion Robotics. I have worked with a wide range of programming languages and frameworks, honing my skills in ROS2, Gazebo, IsaacSim, ArduPilot, and many more. My journey has not only been technical but also fruitful in terms of achievements, as I secured a notable position in competitions such as Teknofest 2022 and Tubitak 2242. I strive to learn and grow while contributing my skills and knowledge to interesting and challenging projects.
