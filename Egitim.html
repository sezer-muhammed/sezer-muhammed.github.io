Robotics

Our society at the Middle East Technical University (METU) has a strong focus on robotics. We have delivered a series of courses aimed at providing a solid foundation in the field of robotics. Our comprehensive coursework encompasses a variety of topics such as Arduino programming, the C programming language, motor driving, and basic control systems.

These courses were designed with the intent of imparting practical knowledge and fostering enthusiasm for robotics among students. We're proud to report that our efforts have reached a substantial number of students; approximately 500 students have benefited from these courses.

Our mission is to continue offering these educational opportunities, hoping to inspire more students to explore the exciting field of robotics.
